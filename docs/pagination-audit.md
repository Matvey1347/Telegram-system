## Notes

- The heaviest architectural change is the Telegram channel analytics page: `analytics` no longer transports posts, invite links, campaigns or invite-link history.
- Frontend consumers now have explicit paginated methods available in `apps/web/src/lib/api.ts`, while compatibility `list()` helpers keep older pages working by unwrapping `items`.
- Managed-post detail splitting, compact options/search endpoints for large dropdowns, and broader page-by-page pagination rollout remain the next follow-up slice.
